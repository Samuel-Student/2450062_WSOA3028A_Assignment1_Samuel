Profile

Dumb_Waifu is a visual artists working mostly in the medium of photography. Their work attempts to capture the nostalgia of the early 2000's and the emerging digital era of the time. Their work can be described with surrealist detachment and an aesthetic of sleeze.They first began working in the medium of photography in mid-late 2022, prior to that they worked primarily as an illustrator.
